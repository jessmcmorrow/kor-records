Band Together

Regular jam sessions with young people aged fourteen to twenty-five who want to form a band and develop their musical interests and skills.

School and Community Projects

Working with a class or existing community group to explore musical improvisation and performance.

SWITCH

A day-long experimental music event for people interested in music, noise, digital technologies and Doing It Yourself. Workshops and gigs in exciting, accessible venues.

Article heading

We want to offer an alternative to the fame and money-driven commercial music industry. We want to offer the experience of being in a band and making music to people who are often excluded. We want to experiment with methods of making music collaboratively. We want to increase the number of musicians with additional support needs and their audiences.
